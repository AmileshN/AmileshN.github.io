Why Practice is Important

On this page students and their parents can access educational resources which can assist with learning. Making a commitment to practice is essential to maximize the impact of your learning ability. Practice is the only way to become a proficient in a new skill or behaviour. Students should learn to embrace the discomfort of being beginners in order to continue to grow and improve.
