Our School

Ottawa Tamil Academy of Culture and Technology (OTACT), is the place where your child will learn Tamil Language and Culture in a friendly and social atmosphere. Our school was established in 1996 and continues to be the Premier Language and Cultural Learning Centre for Tamil Speaking Children in the Nation's Capital, Ottawa, Canada.The main focus in our school is on character building followed by teaching of various skills to help our children achieve their goals in our multicultural Canada. We believe that our Tamil children's goals in life could only be achieved if it is started at the early stage. Instructions at OTACT are given through the medium of mother tongue, Tamil. Our past experience shows that our Tamil Children must have a sound educational basis in their mother tongue, Tamil, so that they can master in either English or any other Languages and chosen Studies at an advanced level. Our school consists of caring students and staff who ensure high student achievement.

Since the creation of our school in 1998 our students have became very successful in their studies achieving numerous achievements.We are proud of our students and their many accomplishments in our years of teaching.
